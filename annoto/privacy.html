Privacy Policy

Annoto · Last updated June 27, 2026

Annoto is built to be private by design. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The screenshots you import, along with any notes, voice memos, and tags you add, are stored only on your device. None of this content is sent to us or to any third party.

On-device processing

Any text recognition or organization happens locally on your device. Your content never leaves your phone through Annoto.

Analytics & tracking

Annoto contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, deleting an item in the app, or deleting the app, permanently removes it.

Children

Annoto is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
