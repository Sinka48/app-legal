Privacy Policy

Brain Snacks · Last updated June 27, 2026

Brain Snacks respects your privacy. We do not require an account and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

What we use

To build your personalized feed, the app uses the topics and interests you select. These selections are sent to our AI provider solely to generate the educational content you see.

Third-party AI processing

Content is generated using a third-party AI service. Your interest selections are processed to create your feed and are not used to identify you.

What we don't do

We do not collect your name, contacts, or location, and we do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data deletion

Your saved items and preferences are stored on your device; deleting the app removes them.

Children

Brain Snacks is intended for general audiences aged 12 and up.
