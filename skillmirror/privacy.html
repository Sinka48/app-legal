Privacy Policy

SkillMirror AI · Last updated July 5, 2026

SkillMirror AI helps you practice job interviews with an AI interviewer. This policy explains how your information is handled.

What you provide

To generate an interview you provide a job description (typed, pasted from a link, or read from a screenshot on your device), and your answers to the interview questions (typed or spoken). Screenshots you add are read for text on your device; the extracted text — not the image — is used for analysis.

How it is processed

Your job description and answers are sent securely to our backend, which forwards them to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) to generate interview questions and your skill report. This processing exists only to provide the app's core features. Voice answers are transcribed on your device; the resulting text is treated the same as typed answers.

Where your data is stored

Your interview history and reports are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts, we do not sell your data, and we do not show ads.

What we don't do

No advertising SDKs, no cross-app tracking, no sharing of your interviews with other users, and your content is not used to train third-party models for unrelated purposes.

Purchases

Optional Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never receive your payment details.

Data deletion

You can delete individual interviews or clear all history in the app. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Children

SkillMirror AI is intended for users aged 12 and up.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
