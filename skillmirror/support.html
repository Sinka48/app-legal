SkillMirror AI Support

SkillMirror AI turns any job description into a realistic AI interview, then gives you a personalized skill report so you walk in prepared.

Getting started

Paste a job posting (text, link, or screenshot), pick an interviewer style, and answer seven questions by typing or speaking. When you finish, tap Generate Skill Report to see your scores, strengths, gaps, and recommended reading.

Premium

The free plan includes three interviews. Premium unlocks unlimited interviews, voice mode, progress tracking, and all interviewer personas. Manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
